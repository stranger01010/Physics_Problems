## 🔊 Question (Echo Ranging)

A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo **1 second later**.  
Speed of sound = **343 m/s**

👉 How far away is the cliff?

---

## 🧠 Concept

The sound:
- travels to the cliff  
- then reflects and comes back  

So the total distance is:
**going + returning**

This means the given time is for the **round trip**, not one way.

---

## 📌 Formula

d = (v × t) / 2

### Why this formula?
- Normally: distance = speed × time  
- But here, sound travels twice the distance (there and back)  
- We only want the one-way distance, so we divide by 2  

---

## 🔢 Solution

### 1. Known values
- v = 343 m/s  
- t = 1 s  

### 2. Substitute into the formula

d = (343 × 1) / 2  

### 3. Calculate

d = 171.5 m  

---

## ✅ Final Answer

👉 The cliff is **171.5 meters away**

---

## 🎯 Short Explanation

> The sound travels to the cliff and back, so the time is for a round trip.  
> To find the one-way distance, we divide by 2.  
> The distance is 171.5 meters.
