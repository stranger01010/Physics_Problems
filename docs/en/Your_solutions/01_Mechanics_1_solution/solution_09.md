# Optimization Problem Solution

## **9. Momentum Comparison Solution**

---

## **Problem**

Which has greater momentum?
- A **2-gram fly** flying at **10 m/s**
- A **60-gram tennis ball** moving at **1 m/s**

---

# **Step 1: Understand the Momentum Formula**

👉 Momentum ($p$) is the product of an object's mass and its velocity:
$$p = m \cdot v$$

---

## 🔍 What does this formula tell us?

- **$m$**: Mass (How much "stuff" is moving)
- **$v$**: Velocity (How fast it is moving)
- **$p$**: Momentum (The "strength" or "oomph" of the motion)

---

## 🧠 WHY is the unit important?

- In physics, we usually use **kilograms (kg)** for mass and **meters per second (m/s)** for velocity.
- Our objects are in **grams**, so we must convert them to **kg** to get the standard unit ($kg \cdot m/s$).

👉 **Conversion:** $1 \text{ gram} = 0.001 \text{ kg}$

---

# **Step 2: Calculate the Momentum of the Fly**

### 👉 Data:
- $m_{fly} = 2 \text{ g} = 0.002 \text{ kg}$
- $v_{fly} = 10 \text{ m/s}$

### Calculation:
$$p_{fly} = 0.002 \cdot 10$$
$$p_{fly} = 0.02 \, \text{kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$$

---

# **Step 3: Calculate the Momentum of the Tennis Ball**

### 👉 Data:
- $m_{ball} = 60 \text{ g} = 0.060 \text{ kg}$
- $v_{ball} = 1 \text{ m/s}$

### Calculation:
$$p_{ball} = 0.060 \cdot 1$$
$$p_{ball} = 0.06 \, \text{kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$$

---

# **Step 4: Compare the Results**

Now we look at the numbers side-by-side:
- **Fly:** $0.02 \, \text{kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$
- **Tennis Ball:** $0.06 \, \text{kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$

---

## 🧠 WHY is the tennis ball's momentum higher?

- Even though the fly is moving **10 times faster** than the ball...
- The tennis ball is **30 times heavier** than the fly.
- In this case, the **mass advantage** of the ball is much stronger than the **speed advantage** of the fly.

---

## ✅ RESULT

👉 The **60-gram tennis ball** has greater momentum.

---

# **🔥 FINAL SUMMARY**

- Momentum depends on both mass and speed.
- A slow but heavy object can easily have more momentum than a fast but light object.
- **Tennis Ball Momentum ($0.06$) > Fly Momentum ($0.02$)**

👉 **Winner:** The Tennis Ball! 🎾
