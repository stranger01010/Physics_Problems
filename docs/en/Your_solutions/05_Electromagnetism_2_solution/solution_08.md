## 📡 Question (Electromagnetic Spectrum)

List the following types of electromagnetic radiation in order of increasing wavelength:

- Infrared  
- Ultraviolet  
- Microwaves  
- X-rays  
- Radio waves  
- Gamma rays  

---

## 🧠 What is this problem about?

This problem is about the **electromagnetic spectrum**.

The electromagnetic spectrum is the complete range of electromagnetic waves arranged according to their:

- wavelength  
- frequency  
- energy  

---

## ❓ What is the question asking?

The question asks us to arrange the waves in order of:

👉 **increasing wavelength**

This means:

- start with the **smallest wavelength**
- end with the **largest wavelength**

---

## 📌 Important Concept

In the electromagnetic spectrum:

- smaller wavelength → higher frequency and higher energy  
- larger wavelength → lower frequency and lower energy  

---

## 🔍 Types of electromagnetic waves

### 🔸 Gamma rays
- Smallest wavelength  
- Highest energy  

### 🔸 X-rays
- Slightly larger wavelength than gamma rays  

### 🔸 Ultraviolet (UV)
- Larger wavelength than X-rays  

### 🔸 Infrared (IR)
- Larger wavelength than visible light  

### 🔸 Microwaves
- Larger wavelength than infrared  

### 🔸 Radio waves
- Largest wavelength  

---

## 🔢 Correct Order (Increasing Wavelength)

Gamma rays → X-rays → Ultraviolet → Infrared → Microwaves → Radio waves

---

## ✅ Final Answer

👉 Gamma rays → X-rays → Ultraviolet → Infrared → Microwaves → Radio waves

---

## 🎤 Short Explanation

> The electromagnetic spectrum is arranged according to wavelength and frequency.  
> Gamma rays have the smallest wavelength, while radio waves have the largest wavelength.  
> Therefore, the correct order is: Gamma rays, X-rays, Ultraviolet, Infrared, Microwaves, and Radio waves.
